This year will include talks from a wide selection of the community

Reproducible research: save your time, save your sanity Sam Hames @scmHames Technical Lead at Kapiche (www.kapiche.com). Software development, text analytics, machine learning, medical imaging PhD candidate
Explaining things clearly: design tricks by a non-designer Mark Hooper @mark_a_hooper Mark is Education and Cultural Change Coordinator for the Office of Research Ethics and Integrity at QUT. His PhD was about the philosophy of David Hume. He designs face-to-face and online training courses, and promotes research integrity. He spends a lot of time thinking about how to explain complex concepts more clearly.
